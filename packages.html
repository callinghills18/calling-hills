Arrival at NJP/Bagdogra. Meet & greet by CallingHills representative and begin your scenic journey towards Darjeeling, with en-route Kurseong sightseeing. Check-in and relax.

  • Hanuman Tak View Point

Day 2 — Darjeeling Leisure Day
Wake up to the magnificent Himalayan atmosphere and Kanchenjunga views. Enjoy a free day for leisure activities around the Queen of Hills.

  • Observatory Hill & Local Tea Tasting
  • Café Hopping & Himalayan Sunset Experience
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Day 3 — Darjeeling to NJP / Bagdogra Drop
Check-out after breakfast and proceed towards NJP/Bagdogra via the scenic Mirik route.
  • Lepchajagat & Simana View Point
  • Indo-Nepal Border & Pashupati Market
  • Gopaldhara Tea Estate View Point
  • Mirik Lake & Town
Meals Included: Breakfast
